Online Registration for ICEPHSS’25

Online Registration for IEEE ICBC 2024 is now open. Before registering for the conference, please read the following policies and procedures.

If you are ready to start the registration process, click on the button below.

Start Registration

POLICIES AND PROCEDURES

All participants must register and pay the appropriate fee in order to participate and access the ICEPHSS 2025 program.

REGISTRATION TRANSFER POLICY:

All requests for registration transfers must be submitted in writing and emailed to j.le@comsoc.org by 24 May, 2024. There will be a US$75 transfer fee imposed and possible additional fees depending upon the new registrant's membership status for each request.

CANCELLATION POLICY:

All cancellation and refund requests must be submitted in writing and emailed to j.le@comsoc.org by 24 May 2024. There will be an administrative fee of US$100 deducted from each refund.

AUTHOR REGISTRATION REQUIREMENTS:

Main Conference Authors must be registered by 22 March 2024 in order to upload final main conference paper.

Workshop Authors must be registered by 12 April 2024 in order to upload final workshop paper.

Authors who have an accepted paper(s) must register for the “Main Conference” by 22 March. Once registration is done, authors will receive a paper upload code that can be used to upload the main conference final paper(s). Please do not share your registration confirmation code with anyone else. Although the code enables submission of up to three final main conference papers, it can only be used once, by you, to create an author account for these submissions.

For more information, feel free to contact us via email or visit the registration page.
