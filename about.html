About The Conference

The International Conference on Evolving Perspectives in Humanities and Social Sciences: Theory, Practice, and Transformation (ICEPHSS’25) is a premier global forum designed to foster interdisciplinary collaboration and innovative thinking. This conference will explore how contemporary challenges, such as technological advancements, globalization, and cultural shifts, are impacting and transforming the humanities and social sciences.

ICEPHSS’25 aims to bridge the gap between theory and practice by bringing together a diverse audience of scholars, researchers, educators, practitioners, and policymakers. The event will provide a platform for presenting cutting-edge research, sharing innovative methodologies, and discussing actionable solutions for pressing global issues.

What makes ICEPHSS’25 truly unique is its holistic approach. The conference incorporates underrepresented yet crucial areas, such as culinary heritage and sustainable food practices, alongside traditional disciplines like literature, linguistics, and critical theories. Moreover, it emphasizes practical applications of digital technologies, equity in education, and the preservation of cultural and historical legacies.

Participants will benefit from engaging keynote speeches, interactive sessions, and collaborative networking opportunities. ICEPHSS’25 is not just an academic gathering—it is a transformative space aimed at driving meaningful change across disciplines and societies.
